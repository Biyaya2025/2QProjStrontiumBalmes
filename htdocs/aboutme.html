Hello! I'm Biyaya Balmes, a 15-year old PSHS student learning how to draw. I was born July 22, 2006 and I started drawing this year!

Here, you can see some of my best digital and pencil art, learn a bit more about me and my interests, and find good art resources I use!

I do my pencil drawings on a sketchpad my mom gave me. I use Krita to create my digital art.

What made me want to start drawing was Ninomae Inanis' drawing streams. Seeing her art, I felt inspired and taught myself using Pinterest and Youtube drawing tutorials!

I mainly draw fanart of virtual youtubers from Nijisanji English and Hololive, Genshin Impact, and my own original characters.

My art inspirations are Ninomae Inanis, LAM, Mika Pikazo, and KeenBiscuit.

I hope to get better and keep on exploring and creating!

Thank you for visiting!
